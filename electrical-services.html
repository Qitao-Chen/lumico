Electrical Repairs & Maintenance For Homes & Business

Our professional expertise extends well beyond just solar, with a comprehensive electrical and maintenance service for residential homes, as well as commercial and industrial properties. That means we can support our customers with quality electrical solutions and ongoing aftercare, so you’ll only ever need to deal with the one electrical contractor.

We specialise in all types of electrical repairs and maintenance, including quick response residential electrical jobs, property renovations, house wiring/rewiring, electrical inspections, air conditioning, security camera system installs, commercial fit outs, and innovative lighting design, installation and servicing. The best way to help keep buildings safe and power systems functioning correctly is with regular maintenance checks, which will also save you money over the long-term.

Our team at Lumico Solar & Electrical will always go out of our way to deliver quality workmanship and innovative thinking for all size budgets and projects. Whether you require a large electrical installation or a minor electrical repair, our team of friendly and experienced technicians can get the job done to suit your specific needs and our fully qualified electrical contractors are available at any time to provide superior service with up-front and honest rates.

We are fully insured for all aspects of electrical and solar contracting, and we work together with our customers to ensure everything is taken care of quickly and safely. From faulty power points or damaged wiring, to complete house blackouts, Lumico Solar & Electrical have electricians available to assist. Whatever your next electrical project is, you can count on the Lumico team of sparkies to get the job done efficiently, on time, and on budget every time.
